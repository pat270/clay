--- title: Form Group Autofit section: Chema Demo ---

Form Group Autofit equally spaces each item by row. You can drop several inputs into this component and expect it to be spaced evenly. The big benefit is not having to rely on Bootstrap's grid and tab order is left to right instead of top to bottom with grids.

In screen sizes less than 576px, they will stack. There are no responsive stacking helpers at the moment, but we plan on adding them.

You can set a max-width on a `.form-group-item` or several and the remaining will expand to fill the space.

There is also support for help text and labels. The only thing that isn't supported are labels that break to new lines.

Horizontal Forms are easier to create with the `form-group-autofit` pattern, just wrap your label in `form-group-item form-group-item-label` and set a max-width on them.
